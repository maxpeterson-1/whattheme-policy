Privacy Policy for WhatTheme: Shopify Theme Detector

Last Updated: July 6, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of information for the "WhatTheme: Shopify Theme Detector" Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

Our core principle is to respect your privacy. We have designed the Extension to be completely private and secure.

2. Data Collection

We collect zero user data.

The Extension operates entirely on your local computer. It analyzes the code of the webpage you are currently visiting to identify the Shopify theme. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or any other data from your device or Google account.

3. Data Usage

Since no data is collected, no data is used for any purpose.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since no data is collected, there is no data to share or disclose to any third parties.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by visiting the support section of our Chrome Web Store listing.
